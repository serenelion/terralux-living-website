Frequently Asked Questions

When does my subscription start?

Your $29 pre-sale payment locks in your founding member status and grants immediate community access. Your 2-month Scout subscription period begins when the TerraLux Home App officially launches (estimated 8-12 weeks from pre-sale close).

What do I get before the app launches?

Immediate community access: virtual events, educational content, build-in-public updates, early announcements for new locations, and access to the global benefits network. You'll be part of the founding conversation as we build.

How do the plant sensors work?

When the app launches, you can order $15 soil sensors and air quality monitors (sold separately). Plants become early warning systems‚Äîmonitoring soil moisture, nutrients, light, and air quality. The app creates a digital twin of your home's wellness environment.

Can I upgrade to higher tiers later?

Yes! All founding Scout members get priority access and preferred pricing when Rider ($360/mo) and Steward ($1,000/mo) tiers activate. As physical locations open, you'll be first in line.

Is membership really curated?

Yes. TerraLux Living is approval-based to ensure community alignment. We review all applications to maintain quality, intimacy, and shared values. If membership is not approved, we issue a full refund.

What happens after my 2-month pre-sale period?

Your membership renews at $29/month or you can switch to the annual plan at $290/year (17% savings). You can cancel anytime‚Äîno long-term commitment required. Your founding member status remains permanent.

Launching 2026-27: Monaco, Santa Barbara, Atlanta.

Why only 1,000 founding Scouts?

We're intentionally limiting the founding cohort to build a tight-knit community, ensure high-quality onboarding, and validate the model before scaling. Early members shape the culture and receive permanent recognition + priority access to all future additions.
